# 05. Brand Guidelines

## Design Philosophy
The website should feel like a modern, premium football competition platform.
- **Aesthetic**: Professional, Clean, Fast.
- **Layout**: Mobile-first and responsive across desktop, tablet, and mobile.
- **Interface**: Minimalist with a strict focus on presenting tournament information clearly.
- **Interactions**: Consistent use of cards, tables, and subtle hover animations to make the platform feel alive without being overwhelming.

## Color Palette
- **Primary Theme**: Dark mode by default.
- **Backgrounds**: Deep, rich dark tones (e.g., dark grays or near-blacks).
- **Brand Accents**: White and Dark Purple.
  - Dark Purple is used for primary buttons, highlights, active states, and subtle gradients.
  - White is used for high-contrast typography, icons, and borders.

## Typography
- Modern, sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or Outfit) to ensure readability for statistics and data tables.
- Heavy font weights for scores and numbers.
- Subdued grays for secondary information (e.g., positions, match dates).
